Removing Garlic Smell from Hands

To remove the smell of garlic from your hands, rub them with stainless steel like a spoon under cold water.

Watch this video to learn how to remove the garlic smell from hands:

Step By Step Guide:

    • Step 1: Wash your hands with soap and water immediately after handling garlic.
      Step 2: Rub your hands with stainless steel. You can use a stainless steel spoon, sink, or special stainless steel soap.
      Step 3: Create a paste using equal parts of baking soda and water. Rub this paste over your hands and rinse with water.
      Step 4: Rub your hands with lemon juice or a slice of lemon to neutralize the garlic odor.
      Step 5: Finish by washing your hands with soap and water again.
