Kitchen Hacks

1. Removing Garlic Smell from Hands

To remove the smell of garlic from your hands, rub them with stainless steel like a spoon under cold water.

2. Easy Peel Hard Boiled Eggs

Add a teaspoon of baking soda to the water when boiling eggs to make them easier to peel.

Home Improvement Hacks

1. Wall Nail Trick

Before hammering a nail into the wall, place a small piece of painter's tape over the spot to prevent paint chips.

2. Furniture Scratch Fix

Use a walnut to rub out scratches on wooden furniture. The natural oils will help restore the wood's appearance.

Tech Hacks

1. Smartphone Stand

Use a paperclip to create a makeshift stand for your smartphone while watching videos.

2. Keyboard Cleaning

Use a sticky note to clean your keyboard. The adhesive will pick up dirt and dust between the keys.
